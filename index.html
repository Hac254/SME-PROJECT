Success Stories

See how we've helped Kenyan businesses thrive

Eco-Friendly Packaging Solutions

Green Pack Kenya

With financial support and mentorship from KU SME Support, Green Pack Kenya developed biodegradable packaging solutions that are now used by major retailers across East Africa.

"The innovation grant and expert guidance helped us scale our production and reach new markets we never thought possible." - Sarah Mwangi, Founder

Digital Healthcare Platform

AfyaConnect

AfyaConnect revolutionized healthcare access in rural Kenya through their mobile platform. KU SME Support provided technical expertise and funding to help them expand their services.

"The mentorship program connected us with healthcare experts who helped refine our business model and technology stack." - James Odhiambo, CEO

Sustainable Agriculture Tech

FarmTech Solutions

FarmTech developed affordable irrigation systems for small-scale farmers. With KU SME Support's innovation program, they were able to prototype and test their solutions in various regions.

"The recognition we received through KU's program opened doors to international partnerships and investment." - Lucy Kamau, Co-founder
